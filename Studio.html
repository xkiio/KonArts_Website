The skill to draw consistently within a specific art style is progressive; yet not impossible. Throughout a person's artistic journey, they can take courses that range from one year to over a decade. Within the education period, animators will be able to learn the basics of animating: body movements, perspective, dynamics, applying motion and phases.

While also understanding the skill of drawing, animators are taught what makes an animated film appealing, and what interesting methods and strategies can be applied to their own drawings.

Regarding applications, many users tend to use softwares such as Moho (Anime Studio) Pro, Blender, Clip Studio Paint, Adobe Animate, and Toon Boom Animate for animating— during the years of education, assessment standards on animation is mostly based individually. In careers, animators work in large groups, and the conceniency of sending files on such softwares are very helpful, it makes the expenses worth paying for.

Everyone has their own individual style, regardless of where their inspiration had been taken source from. In every journey, everyone has their own plan or method to get to their goal— we are only here to help you achieve that goal. Konarts may answer any questions or concerns you have about your plans for your animating career, and we will give you the necessary resources to help you improve.
